certified project for the Essilor Company

  • During one semester
  • Location : Rouen (France)
  • part-time (15-20 hours per week)

In collaboration with INSA Rouen, I took part, in a team of 8 other students, in a machine learning oriented project for Essilor, leader in ophtalmologic optics.

The objective of this project was to create a model of face landmarks adapted to specific needs (can't say more due to confidentiality agreements). In particular, we organized a large-scale data collection campaign, and this data was used to create a machine learning model that could be integrated into the client's architecture.

In this project, we used the scrum-methodology, and we were in close communication with our clients.
